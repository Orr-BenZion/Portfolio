KNOWLEDGE REPRESENTATION COURSE

Infographics of a classmate

In this project I was required to capture the most prominent characteristics of a random classmate.
The subject of my project was Shir, Shir has a unique trait, she's anosmic (a person who's unable to smell).
In this portrait I've depicted her inability to smell by creating a contrast, on one side, a fragrant rose projecting its scent, and on the other, a large clothespin pinching her nose shut. The icons around and above her head are there in order to symbolize relevant traits of being the anosmic person that she is.
